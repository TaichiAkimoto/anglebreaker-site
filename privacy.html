AngleBreaker Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Overview

AngleBreaker ("the App") is a basketball shot analysis tool. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data.

Data We Collect

Camera and Video Frames: The App processes camera frames and video files on your device to detect basketball shots. When performing VLM shot analysis, downscaled frame grids are sent to Google's Gemini API for angle refinement. These frames are processed as temporary API requests and are not stored on any server.

Shot Analysis Data: Shot angles, grades, session scores, and daily streak data are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

Purchase Information: If you subscribe to AngleBreaker Pro, Apple handles all payment processing through StoreKit. We do not have access to your payment information.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No account creation or login required
  • No location data
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No data shared with third parties for advertising purposes

Third-Party Services

Google Gemini API: Downscaled camera frame grids (320px width) are sent to Google's Gemini API for shot timing analysis. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests. Google processes these as ephemeral API calls per their API Terms of Service.

Apple StoreKit: Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple. Apple's privacy policy governs payment data.

Data Storage

All app data (shot history, session scores, streak, preferences) is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. No cloud sync, backup, or server-side storage is performed by the App.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
